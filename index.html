About Us

Your Friend in India

पाल (pāla, “protector, guard, close friend”) - NRI Pal is your friend in India. It was started by an NRI team, who like many Indians settled abroad and often have to rely on friends and relatives back in India for any work they need, but sometimes it's difficult to find someone who can help.

Having personally experienced the issues, we would like our team to serve the NRI community to fulfil any requirements they have back in India in a trusted way.

We can help with any requirement you have in India right from Property management, Documentation or concierge services, you can contact us for anything and no job is a small job for us.

Team in UK & India

We are NRIs settled in UK and have a team in India currently in Hyderabad and looking to serve in other places soon.

Our priority is your happiness working together with us. We guarantee you the best service quality from us. Feel free to talk to us via contact us form, email, Twitter, or Facebook.
